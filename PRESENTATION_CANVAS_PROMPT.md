## Tips for Using Canvas AI
Note: This presentation is designed for exactly **12 minutes** of speaking (Slide 1: 1 min intro, Slides 2-3: 1 min, Slides 4-7: 3 min tech, Slides 8-12: 4.5 min improvements/achievements, Slides 13-14: 1.5 min challenges, Slides 15-17: 2 min conclusions/impact).

1. Copy the **Canvas AI Master Prompt** section into Canvas AI
2. For each slide, customize with:
   - Your specific project details
   - Screenshots from your application
   - Real data from your testing results
   - Actual metrics from your project

3. Design preferences to request:
   - Color scheme: Professional tech-focused (blues, grays, accents)
   - Font: Modern sans-serif (e.g., Helvetica, Roboto)
   - Visual style: Minimalist with emphasis on data visualization

4. Delivery format: Request PPTX for compatibility with all presentation tools

5. Review and customize each slide to ensure accuracy of your specific metrics and examples
